WHO WE ARE?

Cawoca Company Limited was established on the fundamental principles of teamwork, integrity, and unwavering commitment. Presently, with our extensive nationwide presence, our dedicated workforce remains steadfast in upholding the visionary ideals set forth by our founders, ensuring the provision of indispensable services to our esteemed clients, fostering robust community partnerships, and facilitating the delivery of essential resources.

WHAT WE DO!

Our people bring their technical knowledge, experience, and resourcefulness to the delivery of our construction services. Cawoca Company Limited is a leader in all major market segments including healthcare, education, commercial, retail and green building. Driven by a commitment to continuous improvement, Cawoca Company Limited embraces Building Information Modeling and lean construction methods to improve efficiency and enhance client value.


CAWOCA'S CORE VALUES

TeamWork

We recognize our primary asset is people. To be successful, Cawoca Company Limited must be a rewarding place to work. We provide opportunity and encouragement to help our people reach their potential. We work with our clients as a team. Mutual respect provides the foundation for our success.

Integrity

We remain true to our founding values of quality, honesty and hard work. We have the highest ethical standards in the industry. We do “the right thing”. Cawoca Company Limited is consistently associated with the high standards of service, quality and personal attention to clients and integrity.

Commitment

We are proactive in finding solutions for our clients that best achieve their goals. We understand that lasting relationships are the lifeblood of our business so we pay personal attention to our clients by focusing on them as individuals.

LOGISTICS

Cowaca is a Kenyan company with a growing continental footprint, ensuring that it understands and works within the parameters of local conditions, managing all risks effectively; thus giving clients peace of mind through quality delivery on time, whatever the challenges. Cowaca has the ability to swiftly redeploy resources between counties of operation to meet clients’ requirements and to optimize all opportunities to suit stakeholder strategies. Cowaca operates in all the 47 counties and has a focus on establishing a firm presence in Kenya and beyond
